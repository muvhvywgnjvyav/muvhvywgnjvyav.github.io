*camp and go slow* MADE MUSETTE black

カラー　black
製造　swift industries / made in USA
パッチ　Falls Creek Outfitters
素材　cordura

When you’re working on a bag for the inaugural MADE bicycle show, a run-of-the-mill musette just won’t do. So we asked our buds at Swift Industries to help us whip up something special. These bags were cut and sewn in-house at Swift so you know they’re fully finished and built to last. The Field Tan and Olive bags are made from a waxed cotton/Cordura blend, and the Rust and Black bags are built with trusty 1000D Cordura. YKK zips keeps your goods secure, the shoulder strap is fully adjustable, and there’s even a removable sternum cord to keep things steady while you’re riding.
