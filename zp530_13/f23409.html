Wrapping papers by Groovisions, a Japanese design group famous for "Chappie" and other products.

The size is 84cm x 59cm.I had it framed and displayed as art, as shown in the photo.

A set of 3 unused sheets. Those stored in a place out of the sun since I acquired them, so they are in new, unused condition with no tanning, no wrinkles and with vivid colors.

These are quite rare items, and I believe Groovisions fans and collectors out there will love this item.
